How to use full text search
Learn to create simple and complex searches. Hover over a row to see the example search results highlighted below.
Tool Example Explanation
Single word business Exact match of the word
Wildcard "*" business* Match various forms of a word
Multiple words business plan Matches the first AND the second word, can be separated.
Phrase in quotes "business plan" Both the first word and the second must be present, and next to each other
OR words business* OR plan Matches first word AND second word, but order and placement don't matter
Exclude words plan NOT business Matching other words, but excluded word cannot be present
Advanced business AND (plan OR strategy) NOT must Create combinations of required and excluded words and phrases
Example Meeting Text
You must plan your business strategy.
We have a business plan.
There are other businesses competing in our space.
